Aarti Sunder

Aarti Sunder

Multimedia Contemporary Artist

"Art is where perception meets reflection."

Aarti Sunder is a contemporary Indian artist whose work explores the intersections of technology, identity, and human perception. Through digital media, video, and drawing, she challenges the boundaries between the virtual and the real, inviting viewers to question how we experience the modern world.
