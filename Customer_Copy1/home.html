Our Artists

Aarti Sunder

"Art is a dialogue between the seen and the unseen."

Aarti Sunder is a multidisciplinary Indian artist working across video, text, and installation. Her work examines how technology, communication, and perception shape human relationships and emotions. Through minimal visuals and layered narratives, she invites viewers to question what it means to “feel” in the digital age.

Julie Curtiss

"I like to reveal the uncanny in the everyday."

Julie Curtiss is a French-born, Brooklyn-based artist celebrated for her surreal, humorous, and feminist imagery. Her paintings and sculptures explore the tension between beauty and absurdity, often focusing on the mysterious, fragmented nature of women’s identities and modern life.

Anjolie Ela Menon

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see."

Anjolie Ela Menon is one of India’s most acclaimed contemporary painters known for her luminous portraits and ethereal figures. Her work combines Byzantine influences with Indian sensibilities, exploring themes of identity, femininity, and emotion through layered textures and translucent colors.
